Machining Division

First Way Marine Machines & Equipment Maintenance specializes in the servicing, repair, and refurbishment of electric motors, generators, and alternators for marine, offshore, and industrial applications.

Our team of skilled technicians provides both on-board and workshop-based solutions, ensuring reliable performance, operational safety, and reduced downtime for critical power systems.

  • On-board servicing of motors and alternators

  • Rewinding of motors and generators

  • Troubleshooting of motors, generators, and alternators

  • Renew varnishing and baking of motors and generators

  • Electrical insulation testing and performance checks

  • Onshore and offshore service support
